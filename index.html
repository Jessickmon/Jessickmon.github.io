Research

History as a data science:
Missing data imputation on the the slave voyages dataset

Machine Learning Research

A case study on the world's largest database of forced slave relocations explored with the aid of machine learning libraries such as Scitkit-learn, Pandas, Numpy, and Matplotlib employed alongside Python and SQL. This research project aimed to impute missing data using univariate and multivariate techniques, exploiting correlations between the attributes and Jaccard similarities of voyages.
